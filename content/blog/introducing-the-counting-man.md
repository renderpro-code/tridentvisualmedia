He walked into every room and took inventory — the people, the exits, the small wrong details most people never notice. It made him a brilliant film producer, a careful husband, a present father. It also made him an invisible criminal.

Then Detective K. Miller arrested him in a mall food court, in front of his wife and children, and the counting began to unravel.

**The Counting Man** is the first novel in the Miller Crime Thriller series — a literary crime thriller that moves between Robert's meticulous unraveling and Miller's relentless reconstruction, building toward a reckoning neither of them fully anticipated.

A high-dollar crypto theft. An affair that didn't stay contained. And a man who could calculate every variable except the one that mattered most.

It's a study in compartmentalization, in the stories brilliant people tell themselves, and in what happens to the people left behind when those stories finally collapse.
